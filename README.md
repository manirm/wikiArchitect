# WikiArchitect 🏛️
### Your Personal Knowledge, Augmented by Private AI.

WikiArchitect is a local-first, intelligence-driven knowledge engine. It transforms your disparate notes, PDFs, and articles into a cohesive, navigable digital wiki—all while keeping your data **100% private** on your own machine.

---

## 🌟 Why WikiArchitect?

Traditional wikis require you to do all the heavy lifting—manual linking, cataloging, and summarizing. WikiArchitect introduces the **Autonomous Librarian**, a local AI that works alongside you to:
- **Connect the Dots**: Automatically identify relationships between your files.
- **Synthesize Insights**: Generate summaries and structural "Maps of Content" from your raw data.
- **Keep You Organized**: Perform background maintenance to ensure your knowledge base stays healthy and discoverable.

## 🛡️ Privacy First, Always
In an era of cloud-hosted AI, WikiArchitect stands for **Data Sovereignty**.
- **Local LLMs**: Powered by [Ollama](https://ollama.com/), the AI processing happens on *your* CPU/GPU.
- **No Cloud Required**: Your files never leave your `/vault/` folder.
- **Offline Capable**: Research and organize even without an internet connection.

## 🚀 Core Features
- 🧠 **Semantic Assistant**: Chat with your vault. Ask complex questions and get answers based *only* on your notes.
- 🗺️ **Knowledge Graph**: Visualize the emergent structure of your thinking in a dynamic D3.js graph.
- ⚖️ **Architect Review**: You remain in control. Review every AI-proposed change in a side-by-side "Difference Viewer" before it's saved.
- 📦 **Cross-Platform**: Professional installers for Windows (EXE), macOS (DMG), and Linux (AppImage).

---

## 🏁 Quick Start
1. **Install Ollama**: Download from [ollama.com](https://ollama.com).
2. **Pull the Librarian**: Run `ollama pull gemma4-pro:latest`.
3. **Launch**: Open WikiArchitect and click the **Ingest (+)** button to add your first PDF.
