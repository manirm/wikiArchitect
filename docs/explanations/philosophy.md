# The Librarian & The Architect
### A Philosophy of Human-AI Collaboration.

WikiArchitect is built on the belief that AI should be a high-speed research partner, not a final decision-maker. We implement this through two distinct roles.

---

## 📚 The Librarian (The AI)
The Librarian’s job is to manage the **Complexity of Discovery**.
- **Infinite Reading**: The Librarian can "power-read" a 100-page PDF in seconds.
- **Pattern Matching**: It identifies connections between a note you wrote today and one you wrote six months ago.
- **Structural Proposal**: It suggests where links should go and how the vault should be organized.

## 🏛️ The Architect (The Human)
The Architect’s job is to manage the **Context of Truth**.
- **Wisdom**: You understand why two ideas are connected, whereas the AI only sees that they are related.
- **Oversight**: You catch hallucinations and ensure the AI hasn't made a logical leap that isn't true.
- **Approval**: Through the **Diff Viewer**, you have final authority over every single character written to your wiki.

## 🤝 Building a World-Class Knowledge Base
By separating these roles, WikiArchitect achieves what neither a human nor an AI can do alone:
- **Speed**: Accelerated research and ingestion.
- **Structure**: A self-organizing, navigable knowledge graph.
- **Integrity**: A verified, private, and factual source of truth.
