# Your First Conversation with the Librarian
### Moving beyond storage to synthesis.

Now that you've added your first file, it’s time to see why WikiArchitect is more than just a folder of notes. The Assistant on the right is your collaborator.

---

## 🗨️ The Strategy of Asking
Don't just treat the Assistant as a search engine. Treat it as a research partner who has read your *entire* vault.

### 1. The Synthesis Query
Try asking: *"Based on the notes I've added so far, what are the most common themes?"*
The Librarian will scan your index and provide a high-level summary, citing which notes it retrieved.

### 2. The Connection Discovery
Try asking: *"Are there any connections between [Note A] and [Note B] that I missed?"*
The Assistant will look for overlapping concepts and suggest places where you might want to add a link.

### 3. The New Page Proposal
Try asking: *"What topic is missing from my current research that would tie these together?"*
If the Librarian finds a logical gap, it might propose a **New Wiki Page**. If it does, you can review its proposal and add it to your wiki in one click.
