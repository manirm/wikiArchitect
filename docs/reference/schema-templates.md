# The WikiArchitect Schema System
### Standardizing our knowledge structures.

For the Librarian to be effective, it needs consistent structures to follow. We define these in `vault/schema/`.

---

## 🏛️ MOC_TEMPLATE.md
This template defines the layout of your knowledge entry points. It is located at `vault/schema/MOC_TEMPLATE.md`.
- **Header**: Includes the date and a high-level summary of the knowledge domain.
- **Recent Breakthroughs**: Captures the most important insights from your research.
- **Emergent Clusters**: Automatically categorizes recent notes into themes.
- **Structural Gaps**: Highlights where research is missing or topics remain unexplored.

## 📊 WEEKLY_BRIEFING.md
This template defines how your activities are synthesized.
- **Activity Log Reflection**: A summary of what was ingested and what was modified.
- **The "State of the Vault"**: A strategic narrative of your knowledge evolution.
- **Suggested Next Steps**: AI-driven research goals for the coming week.

---
**Advanced Users:** You can modify these templates in `vault/schema/` to suit your own personal research style. The Librarian will follow your structural adjustments automatically.
